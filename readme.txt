== Installation ==

<p>Installation of <strong>Vubon Hover Effects Wordpress</strong> is very simple.</p>

<strong>Option 1</strong>

<li> Download the plugin vubon-hover-effects.zip from download location.</li>
<li> Unpack the *.zip file and extract the Vubon Hover Effects Wordpress folder.</li>
<li> Drop the Vubon Hover Effects Wordpress plugin folder into your wp-content/plugins folder.</li>
<li> In word press administration panels, click on Plugins from the menu.</li>
<li> You should see your plug-in listed under Inactive plug-in tab.</li>
<li> To turn the word presses plug-in on, click activate.</li>

<strong>Option 2</strong>

<li> Download the plugin Vubon Hover Effects Wordpress from download location.</li>
<li> Go to Add New menu under Plugins tab in your word-press admin.</li>
<li> Select upload link (top link menu).</li>
<li> Upload the available Vubon Hover Effects Wordpress file and click install now</li>
<li> Finally click activate plug-in link to activate the plug-in.</li>

<strong>Settings Page</strong>	

<p>Under <strong>Settings -&gt; Vubon Hover Effects Wordpress Plugin</strong> you can configure the basic settings. Everything else can be done on the fly via Custom post named<strong>Vubon Hover Effects Wordpress</strong> on your Dashboard and via the Shortcode option on any Page or Post that you want.</p>

<p><strong>Add Vubon Hover Effects - </strong>Allows you to add hover feature to Custom Posts as well as add information such as:  Title, Description, Image in the Vubon Hover Effects custom post.</p>


<p><strong>Item Position -</strong> You can select which Hover item you want to appear first.  You can also select all of your items and drag to move the order of each up and down in the Custom Post Panel. </p>

<p>
<strong>Category - </strong>Choose the Category as you want to use for your hover effects.</p>


<strong>How To Use</strong>

<p>When you go to your Wordpress Dashboard you will now see a new custom post named <strong>Vubon Hover Effects </strong>in your left toolbar under 

<strong>pages.</strong> This will allow you to quickly create Vubon Hover Effects item on your site.</p>


<p>

To create an Vubon Hover item all you need is the URL of the image. The image can come from any source and be any size, you can also use images uploaded right from the <strong>Post Media Uploader</strong>.  
Just copy the URL of the image after you upload it</p>

<p>
Another important thing to remember is that the image must always have its width and height set. This is important to maintain the layout of the image wrapper; your site, and to make sure the hover functions properly. Luckily, when using the plugin it will attempt to get these values for you.

</p>

<p>&nbsp;
</p>

<p>
<strong>Using the Vubon Hover Effects Plugin</strong></p>

<p>
1. Once you see custom post name <strong>Vubon Hover Effects </strong>On your Dashboard left under <strong>pages.</strong> just click Add New Item :
	
</p>



<p>
2. You will see the Hover information option panel with <strong>3 fields</strong>
</p>

<p>
3. <strong>First field</strong>: "Upload Image" Click to choose a file, the image can come from any source and be any size, you can also use images uploaded right from the post media uploader. Just copy the URL of the image after you upload it. </p>

<p>
4. <strong>Second field</strong>: "Enter Your Title" You can enter any title, the important thing to remember is that the item width and height is limit so please enter short title. This is important to maintain the layout of the image wrapper.
</p>

<p>
5. <strong>Third field</strong> "Enter Your Description" You can quickly insert the description in the description text area. It is important to enter a short description to maintain the layout of the image wrapper.
</p>


<p>
After all of the steps above are done you will see a Menu named <strong>Add more Vubon Hover Item</strong>. If you want to add more items just click <strong>Add more Vubon Hover Item</strong> and you will see another field open; you can then add unlimited items.

</p>



<p>6. When Vubon Hover Item Created with category support done, we have to add shortCode on post or page where we want to show.</p>



<p>9. Go to <strong>Dashboard</strong>, <strong>Click Ad New Post or page.</strong> </p>



<p>8. In your <strong>Post/Page Editor</strong> you will see a blue button within your editor.</p>


<p>9. Put Any Title On Title field, click the <strong>vubon hover Item Shortcode Button with green Icon</strong>, you will then see a Box open with a <strong>ShortCode Option Panel.</strong>
</p>



<p>10. In the <strong>Shortcode Option Panel</strong> there are 2 fields.</p>



<p>11. <strong>First Field </strong>: "Category Name" Put the same category name which you created before with Vubon Hover Item.  Please remember that the Category Name should be the same as your Hover Item Category.
</p>



<p>12. <strong>Second Field </strong>: "Select Hover Style" There are 5 unique amazing hover styles to choose from.
</p>



<p>Click Insert Button and you will see a Shortcode in your post/page editor. </p>


<p> EX:</p>

<pre>[hover category="hover" style="style1"]</pre>



<p>After All Above all just click <strong>publish </strong></p>


<h2>And See the Magic of All Vubon Hover Effects, Enjoy!</h2>
